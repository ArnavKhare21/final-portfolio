About Me(click me!)

As a final-year B.Tech student at Netaji Subhas University of Technology, I thrive at the intersection of data analytics, product strategy, and management consulting. My experience, from building financial models in Excel to collaborating with BCG consultants on Power BI dashboards, is centered on a single goal: translating complex data into actionable business strategy. Whether it's architecting a predictive churn model in Python to save $1.5M in revenue or authoring a go-to-market strategy for a new app, I am passionate about owning problems end-to-end—from initial analysis to final recommendation.
